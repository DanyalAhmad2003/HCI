Samsung 65in 4K TV

Step 2 of 3: Some other common issues to detect with your TV include:

No Sound:

This can be caused by the TV being on mute, a problem with the speaker, or an external device connected to the audio output.

Distorted Sound:

This can be due to a faulty speaker, a loose cable, or a problem with the TV's audio settings.

Audio Delay:

This can occur if the TV is not receiving the audio signal correctly or if there is a problem with the external speakers.
