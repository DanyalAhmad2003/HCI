MacBook M4 Repair

Step 4 of 4: Check for other common macbook realted issues. Wi-Fi Connectivity: Problems connecting to Wi-Fi can be caused by various factors, including the router, network settings, or even software issues. Charging Issues: A MacBook not charging can be due to a damaged charging port, a faulty charging cable, or a problem with the power adapter. Screen Flickering: Screen flickering can be caused by hardware problems, software glitches, graphics card issues, or even overheating. Overheating: If your MacBook is overheating, it could be due to blocked cooling vents, a faulty fan, or even the age of the battery. Startup Issues: A MacBook that won't turn on or has problems booting up can be due to a variety of issues, including damaged hardware or software problems. No Sound: If you can't hear sound from your MacBook, it could be due to a software glitch, a muted speaker, or a problem with the audio settings. the overall speed of your MacBook device. The device can become slow overtime due to factors like low storage space, too many applications running simultaneously, outdated software, or a cluttered desktop.
