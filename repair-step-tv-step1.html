Repair Guide

Samsung 65in 4K TV

Step 1 of 3: There can be a variety of common issues to detect with your TV.

No Signal/Poor Signal:

This can be due to loose connections, a faulty cable, or a problem with the TV's input source.

Distorted Picture:

Issues like the image being squashed, stretched, or having incorrect aspect ratios can be resolved by adjusting the TV's picture settings, such as zoom or aspect ratio.

Flickering Screen:

This can be caused by various factors, including damaged cables, incorrect refresh rate settings, or software issues.

Black Screen/Blank Display:

This could indicate a problem with the TV's backlight, a loose connection, or a more serious hardware issue.

Remove the broken screen and install a new one, if needed.
