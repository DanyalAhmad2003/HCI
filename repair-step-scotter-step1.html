Repair Guide

RX7 Electric Hyper Scooter

Step 1 of 2: There can be a variety of common issues with your electric scotter. Some include, Dead Battery: The scooter won't turn on or charge, often due to a defective battery, improper charging, or cold weather. Battery Degradation: Batteries lose their ability to hold a charge over time, leading to reduced range and performance. Overcharging: Overcharging can damage lithium-ion batteries and shorten their lifespan.

Battery is a crucial element for your scotter, and hence it should be replaced if you detect the above problems
