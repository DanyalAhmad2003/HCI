Repair Guide

RX7 Electric Hyper Scooter

Step 1 of 2: There can be a variety of common issues with your electric scooter.

Dead Battery:

The scooter won't turn on or charge, often due to a defective battery, improper charging, or cold weather.

Battery Degradation:

Batteries lose their ability to hold a charge over time, leading to reduced range and performance.

Overcharging:

Overcharging can damage lithium-ion batteries and shorten their lifespan.

The battery is a crucial element for your scooter, and it should be replaced if you detect the above problems.
