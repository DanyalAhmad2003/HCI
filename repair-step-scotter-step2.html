Repair Guide

RX7 Electric Hyper Scooter

Step 2 of 2: Some more common issues with your electric scooter include:

Flat Tires:

Punctures or leaks can lead to flat tires. Replace them as needed.

Brake Problems:

Brakes may wear out, requiring replacement of brake pads.

Slow Charging:

Ensure the correct charger is used and check for issues with the charging port.

Motor Issues:

Problems can lead to reduced acceleration, unusual noises, or difficulty climbing hills.

Controller Malfunctions:

The controller can fail to regulate power, leading to poor performance.

Throttle Issues:

The throttle may not respond correctly or cause the scooter to accelerate unpredictably.

If further repairs appear too costly, have no fear and scan your device to find out how to sustainably recycle it!
