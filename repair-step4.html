Step 4 of 4: Another common issue your phone may be experiencing is:

Overheating:

Cause: Overuse, high ambient temperatures, or underlying hardware issues can cause phones to overheat.

Solution: Reduce screen brightness, close unnecessary apps, and avoid using the phone in direct sunlight or while charging.
