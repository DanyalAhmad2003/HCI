Step 4 of 4: Another common issue you phone can be expierencing is Overheating

Cause: Overuse, high ambient temperatures, or underlying hardware issues can cause phones to overheat. Solution: The Big Phone Blog suggests reducing screen brightness, closing unnecessary apps, and avoiding using the phone in direct sunlight or while charging.
